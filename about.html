About

I’m a second-year mechanical engineering student at the University of Vermont with interests in aerospace, renewable energy, sustainability, and manufacturing.
I’ve been using CAD software and 3D printing on my own equipment since I was about 13, and I was heavily involved in competitive robotics throughout high school. At UVM, I started as a technician and I now help run the FabLab, where I manage and operate 3D printers and other fabrication tools, assist students with technical projects, and support university research.
Through these experiences, I have gained extensive firsthand experience designing mechanical systems, working within real constraints, and rapidly prototyping functional parts and assemblies.
Outside of engineering, I enjoy biking, camping, skiing (even though I'm new to it), and spending time with my cat, Godzilla.
